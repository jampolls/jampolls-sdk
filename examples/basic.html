🎯 Jampolls SDK Demo

See how easy it is to embed interactive polls on your website with just a few lines of code!

📋 How to Integrate

Step 1: Include the Jampolls SDK

<script src="https://cdn.jampolls.com/jampolls.min.js"></script>

Step 2: Add a container for your poll

<div id="my-poll"></div>

Step 3: Initialize the widget

<script> JamPolls.embed('your-embed-key', 'my-poll'); </script>

🚀 Basic Integration

Perfect for most websites. Just include the script and initialize with your embed key.

JamPolls.embed('abc123', 'poll-container');

⚙️ Auto-Embed

Add data attributes to automatically embed polls without JavaScript.

<div data-jampolls-embed="abc123"></div>

🎨 Custom Styling

Control the appearance with theme options and custom CSS.

JamPolls.embed('abc123', 'poll', { theme: 'dark', showBranding: false });
