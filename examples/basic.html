Jampolls Embed Examples

Replace YOUR_EMBED_KEY with the key from your Jampolls dashboard.

1 · Auto-embed (data attribute)

<script src="https://cdn.jampolls.com/jampolls.min.js"></script>
<div data-jampolls="YOUR_EMBED_KEY"></div>

2 · Manual init

JamPolls.embed('YOUR_EMBED_KEY', '#poll-manual');

3 · Dark theme + callbacks

JamPolls.embed('YOUR_EMBED_KEY', '#poll-dark', {
  theme: 'dark',
  onLoad: data => console.log('loaded', data),
  onVote: e  => console.log('voted',  e),
  onError: e => console.error(e),
});
